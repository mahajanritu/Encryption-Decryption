About Encryption and Decryption

Encryption is the process of converting plain text into unreadable text using a secret key to prevent unauthorized access.

Decryption is the reverse process, converting encrypted text back into readable form using the key.

We use AES encryption in this project, which is a strong symmetric key encryption method widely used in real-world applications.
