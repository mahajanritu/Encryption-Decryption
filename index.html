About Encryption & Decryption

Encryption is the process of converting plain text into unreadable text using a secret key. This ensures that only authorized users can access the information and keeps sensitive data safe from unauthorized access.

Decryption is the reverse process, converting encrypted text back into readable form using the key. Without the correct key, the message remains scrambled and unreadable.

AES (Advanced Encryption Standard) is a symmetric key encryption algorithm widely used in government, banking, and software applications due to its high security and efficiency.

Encryption can be symmetric (same key for encryption and decryption) or asymmetric (public and private key pair). AES is a symmetric method that is fast and secure for encrypting large amounts of data.

Strong encryption ensures data privacy, secure communication, and protection against cyber attacks. Our project demonstrates how AES encryption can be used to secure your messages efficiently.
